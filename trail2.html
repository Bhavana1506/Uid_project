All about Niacinamide

What is it?

Also known as vitamin B3 and nicotinamide, niacinamide is a water-soluble vitamin that works with the natural substances in your skin to help visibly minimize enlarged pores, tighten lax or stretched out pores, improve uneven skin t, soften fine lines and wrinkles, diminish dullness, and strengthen a weakened surface. Niacinamide also reduces the impact of environmental damage because of its ability to improve skin's barrier (its first line of defense), plus it also plays a role in helping skin to repair signs of past damage.

It helps with

  • Enlarged pores
  • Signs of aging
  • Uneven tone
  • Dullness

Who's it for

  • Normal
  • Dry
  • Oily
  • Combination

When to use

Can be used in the morning and night.Always follow with SPF 30+ during the day.

All about Salicylic Acid

What is it?

Salicylic acid is a naturally occurring molecule derived from the bark of willow trees. It's part of a class of acids called beta-hydroxy acids, or BHAs, and is known for its ability to help exfoliate and slough away dead skin cells from the skin's surface.

It helps with

  • Balckheads and Whiteheads
  • Clogged pores
  • Inflammation
  • Breakouts/Acne

Who's it for

  • Oily
  • Acne-pr skin
  • Combination

When to use

Can be used in the morning and night.Always follow with a broad spectrum sunscreen with SPF of atleast 30 during the day.

All about Hyaluronic Acid

What is it?

Hyaluronic acid is a large molecule produced naturally by your body. In skin care products, hyaluronic acid is used as a humectant — a substance that helps the skin hold on to water. It helps hydrate the outer layers of skin, thereby improving the skin's appearance.

It helps with

  • Hydration
  • Skin texture
  • Skin cell regeneration
  • Skin barrier enhancement

Who's it for

  • Oily
  • Normal
  • Combination
  • Dry
  • Sensitive

When to use

Can be used at day or night.
